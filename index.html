Logitech G102

The Logitech G102 is the perfect budget recommendaton for anyone whether be it casual gaming or general office work. At the price of 1500 Indian Ruppess, it is very tough to beat this.

Specifications

  • Dimentions:
    • Height: 116.6 mm
    • Width: 62.15 mm
    • Depth: 38.2 mm
  • Weight: 85 gm (without wires)
  • Cable Lenght: 2.1 m
  • 200 to 8000 DPI
  • RGB Lighting
  • Also known as G103 and G203
  • Switches: Omron D2FC-F-7N(10M)

Experience

I quite like the overall mouse, It's one of the best in the price range. I like its symmetical classic design and tastefully applied RGB adds to the look. The GHUB software is also quite awesome, you can change the presets and RGB, add shortcuts and change the DPI.
